# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- Customers: Buyers seeking high-quality fresh fruits who need quick search, easy navigation, and seamless checkout.
- Shop Owners: Store managers who register, upload products, track sales, and fulfill orders.
- Delivery Staff: Logistics operators who view pending deliveries, update shipping status, and confirm receipt.
- Admin: Platform coordinators who approve shop registrations, audit compliance, and manage platform health.

## Product Purpose
To provide a fast, secure, and intuitive online marketplace for fruit trading (Ban Hoa Qua Online), matching local shop owners with consumers and managing full order-delivery lifecycles.

## Brand Personality
- Fresh: Vibrant yet harmonious natural accents representing fresh produce.
- Trustworthy: Clear layouts, readable terms, and distinct status updates.
- Effortless: Fast load times, responsive elements, and minimized friction in checkout and dashboard actions.

## Anti-references
- Over-designed glassmorphism or SaaS gradients that distract from the physical product (fruits).
- Cluttered layouts with tiny, unreadable font styling on mobile devices.
- Tiny touch targets on mobile interfaces that lead to accidental clicks.

## Design Principles
1. Mobile-First Responsiveness: Ensure buyers can shop and browse easily on any smartphone.
2. Content Over Decoration: The fruit imagery and pricing are the heroes. Avoid decorative shapes, borders, and shadows.
3. Clean Touch Layouts: Navigation and interactive inputs must have at least a 44px touch target area.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio >= 4.5:1 for body and interaction text.
- Large and clean typography suitable for general consumers.
- Tap indicators and button feedback.
